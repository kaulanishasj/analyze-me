Anisha Kaul's Activity Data from her Nexus 5 Android Phone

Here are some key observation's that I could make from my data:

1. I had quite a nice time in Mario's on 2016-01-29 on a Friday! And then I went for a walk to ellsworth avenue the same night! Quite Interesting :D

2. I spend a lot of time at Hunt Library, Hamburg Hall and Squirrel Hill (This is where I live) and Pretty Much nowhere else (Welcome to CMU, duh!)



1. On Fridays, the device that collects my data suddenly starts detecting my accuracy much better than the rest of the days in the week. I found this by grouping and averaging the values of the 'accuracy' from the data I got. The deifnition of accuracy is defined in the data dictionary provided to us.



1. Well! Turns out not that diligent after all. Most of my data is 'NA', which of course can happen in any data collection campaign.

2. I spend most of my time, Sitting still. Hard to believe that, to be honest!
